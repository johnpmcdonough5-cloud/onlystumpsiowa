FAQ

Winneshiek County stump grinding questions

How much does stump grinding cost in Winneshiek County?

Most stumps in Winneshiek County cost $7 per inch of diameter, measured at the widest point including the root flare, with a $200 minimum per visit. Deep grinding (18–20 inches) adds $2.50 per inch and full cleanup adds $5 per inch. Half-day ($900) and full-day ($1,800) packages are the best value for multiple stumps.

Do you serve Decorah and Calmar?

Yes — we cover all of Winneshiek County, including Decorah, Calmar, Ossian, Spillville and Fort Atkinson. Rural acreages outside town are no problem; our equipment travels anywhere in the county, and quotes are free.

Do you haul away the grindings in Winneshiek County?

Cleanup is optional on every Winneshiek County job. We grind the stump at least 6 inches below grade and pile the grindings neatly — keep them as free mulch, or add haul-away for $5 per inch. You only pay for what you need.
